Welcome to reservation system demo, designed for beauty studio.

This site had been created to demonstrate how the online reservation system written in Django framework will work.

User can make a reservation for one of available services, for the chosen date and for specific employee. In return he gets a confirmation of the reservation he made with unique reservation number.

The reservation is passed to employee calendar where registered employee can display his own calendar with upcomming appoinments. Only admin can create new services and new employees.
